Dental Schools across the country are increasingly using the Objectively Structured Clinical Exam as a standardized test to measure clinical skills acquisition. The University of Michigan, a worldwide leader in Dental Education, recognized that the unwieldy paper-based test was causing frustration for both students and faculty graders. My Instructional Design team was charged with improving the testing experience - and moving everything online.

Institutional change can happen at a dizzying pace in small organizations. What happens when a leading nonprofit in Higher Education undergoes a radical rebrand in the face of GDPR and the California Privacy Law?

For starters, a second look at member data and collection practices is in order. My consulting group conducted a seven-stage interview process with stakeholders to clarify existing data practices and sharing habits across the organization. Digital collections and web content creation are not new ideas to the modern college library. But what does a library with no physical collection look like? It looks like the Taubman Health Sciences Library!

The Taubman Health Sciences Library boasts cutting-edge research tools such as the Anatomage table (as featured on Grey's Anatomy). It also boasts almost 100 separate research guides - portals which assist researchers in navigating the library's extensive digital collections. My team and I used needs assessment and product evaluation to recommend some processes to improve guide layout and overall user experience.
